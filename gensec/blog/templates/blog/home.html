VISION

Hello, I am Nihal Shetty, one of the candidates contesting for the post of General Secretary. I believe, in order to create an administration not based on utopian promises, it is imperative that a platform such as this must exist. On this portal, students can view the current proceedings of matters in the jurisdiction of the General Secretary, as well as provide suggestions/post complaints.

Let's face it, not every problem can be solved. But that does not mean you shouldn't know why.If elected, I intend to address as many as possible grievances/suggestions within my capabilities, and do either of the two - solve it, or tell you why it cannot be solved. Also, to maintain transparency, I shall try my best to keep you updated about the progress of ongoing work.

This elections, let's vote uninfluenced. Let's vote for NITK.
